Ethos

Some of my most impactful early lessons were about intent over precision. Initially the story you're writing matters most, not the spelling of the words. Precision follows with refinement and time. I care about outcomes over process, and I've never bought into the idea that form and function are in competition. The best things sit where they resolve: technically and functionally sharper for the user, and still a delight to use. Simple where complexity would just be showing off.
